AESTHETIC

Most design systems are a component library and a thousand decisions left open. This is one CSS file and seven laws — import it, and the decisions are already made.

  1. 01 One font size. Hierarchy is ink and weight, never scale.
  2. 02 Nine registers. Three inks by three weights, nothing else.
  3. 03 One accent per view. The single thing that matters now.
  4. 04 Screens, not pages. Every view fits the viewport; documents scroll inside the stage.
  5. 05 Motion is feedback. It resolves once and persists, never decorates.
  6. 06 Buttons are not links. They never share a costume.
  7. 07 Light and dark are equals. The system decides; the user overrules.
