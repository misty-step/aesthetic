Method and caveats

Scores are means over 200 trials per hypothesis; intervals are the inter-quartile spread. "Not separable" means the leader's advantage falls within that spread. Cost is per-1k-token inference plus retrieval, normalized to the field median. The latency gate measures the p99 tail under a 3× nominal load spike; nominal-load latency clears for every candidate.
