ARENA · math-reasoning · CONFIDENCE READ

Six candidate compositions, each a mean score and a 95% confidence interval on one shared 0–100 scale. The certification question is the same every time: is the lead inside the spread — are the top two intervals separable? Six ways to draw the interval, restrained ink to expressive field. Swap them; same data, so the overlap reads honestly.

FIG 1 · spread on the glyph

1 · SPREAD ON THE GLYPH — most restrained: the mean in mono, a faint ±range beside it, and a status glyph only when this candidate is statistically separable from the runner-up (intervals clear). Exact and tiny; nothing to spend on the page. Trade-off: no visual field — you read overlap from the numbers, candidate by candidate, not at a glance.
